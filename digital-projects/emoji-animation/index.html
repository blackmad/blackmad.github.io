Emoji Animation

https://github.com/blackmad/emoji-anim

I had an idea that I could use the twitter SVG emoji library to make automated animations out of emojis. I don’t know why procedural animation holds such magic for me.

I had a lot of trouble finding any python library that was happy to read & manipulate these SVGs, so I ended up taking a tip from Darius and just hacking the XML like he did for glitch logos

I don’t love the output, especially since it’s not what I had in mind (I wanted to animate drawing in the outlines and filling them in, but stopped at just drawing individual elements using this hack
