1Q84 Music

Thankfully, lit Twitter has taught me that I’m not the only person whose brain is constantly rhyming song lyrics in their heads, at all times, against their will. This bot was supposed to be an expression of that.

The rhymes ended up not being very good. I think this was in part due to not having good part-of-speech tagging/matching? Like, it’s funnier to replace the “boy” in “little drummer boy” with something that can also be the noun in that sentence. Also, replacing “boy” with “broy” isn’t funnier because the words are too close, and also, what the hell is a “broy”
